What is the
study about?

This research project is investigating people’s attitudes to cybersecurity training videos that may be used in organisations. Through this research, we hope to better understand what influences people’s behaviours when using computers for work purposes.

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to complete a brief questionnaire. You'll also watch various training videos and give some feedback.

  • I'm interested!

Can I take part?

Are you at least 18 years of age, you speak and understand English, you are currently employed, and you use a computer in your workplace?

If so, you can get involved!

The study lasts approximately 60 minutes, and will take place in the School of Psychology at the University of Adelaide on North Terrace.

What are the benefits?

This research hopes to identify and address issues with current workplace training. By taking part you are contributing to improving workplace training offered to others in the future.

All participants receive a $20 Myer Gift card as thanks for taking part.
