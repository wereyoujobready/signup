What is the
study about?

Thank you for your interest in our study. Data collection has now completed.

This research project investigated people’s attitudes to cybersecurity training videos that may be used in organisations. Through this research, we hope to better understand what influences people’s behaviours when using computers for work purposes.

Participants were asked to complete a brief questionnaire, watch a set of cyber security training videos, and gave their feedback in a semi-structured interview. The results will be posted once finalised.
